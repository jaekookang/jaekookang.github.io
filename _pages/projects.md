### Uncontrolled Manifold Simulator 02 (Jan 2019)
- Uncontrolled manifolds (UCM) are crucial for human motor learning. The concept is intuitive, but not the implementation. This demonstrates the movement of three-joint robot arms interactively and its null space as a spiral shape ([Link](https://robot-arm-ucm.herokuapp.com/)).
